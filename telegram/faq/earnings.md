## "Why are different phones on different IPs in the same setup giving wildly different rewards?" — 💬💬💬

**Asked by:** Kyle [Apr 3], Wopdiddly [Apr 26], many others.

Reverse-engineered community answer: phones on **single SIM cards** earn $0.20–0.40/day; phones on **shared WiFi with 5+ devices behind one IP** earn $0.001–0.05/day. Static-IP setups also get penalised. Carrier-shared NAT IPs get penalised.

**Team answer:** Sophie/team confirmed only that "there is indeed a limit in devices/IP" (Apr 1). The actual number has never been published.
