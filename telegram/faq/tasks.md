## "How does Scout & Runner work for me as a regular user?" — 💬💬

**Team answer (Apr 7 announcement + Kay Apr 7):**
- Scouts submit phone-number test reports → IVR confirms → Runners dial those numbers from real SIMs
- Reward split: 96% to runners, 4% to scouts as ongoing finder share, plus 5 UP bonus per validated test for scouts and 0.10 UP per non-successful test
- Top route rate $0.28/verified minute
- Scouts capped at 50 per country (USA recently increased to 300)
- Runners not capped — "thousands per country (170+ countries)"
- A scout can run reports for any number of ULOs; runners can serve multiple scouts
