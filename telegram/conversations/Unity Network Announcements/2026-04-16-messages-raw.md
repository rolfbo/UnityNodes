[Photo] 🤝 **Unity Network - Application & Web Updates**

The Unity Network web panel has received a major upgrade, with new features rolling out across web, Android, and iOS. This update focuses on bringing the web panel closer to feature parity with the app, as well as improvements to license management, communication, and general housekeeping.


Features, improvements, and general updates:
- Messaging is now available directly within the web panel
- Mass messaging for reaching your network efficiently
- Settings menu for the web app
- Pagination on licensing for smoother browsing of larger portfolios
- License search on the marketplace
- License alias support
- Marketplace browsing and claim flow - users can browse and claim before signing in
- Manage your owned licenses and lease life cycle directly from the web panel
- Custom sound for push notifications
- Additional anti-bot protection
- General improvements to web panel performance and stability
