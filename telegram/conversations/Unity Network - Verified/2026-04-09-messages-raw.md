Hi friends. A little late to the party here, but I spent some time going through the AMA and thought I’d share a few observations for anyone catching up. I might not have interpreted everything perfectly, but this is what stood out to me.

Noto Nodes, [Apr 9, 2026 at 10:15]
#1
Telemetry rewards are not being removed

Quote
“Your existing telemetry tasks will continue to run as normal.”

And later:

“rewards for normal devices will restore in due course as recalibration completes”  

Takeaway
The base telemetry layer still exists. Current low rewards are part of the recalibration phase, not a confirmed permanent change to the reward model.

Noto Nodes, [Apr 9, 2026 at 10:16]
#2
The recalibration is tied to abuse detection

Quote
“We had a case where over a number of ULO licenses there were literally more than 10,000 proxies being run.”

Takeaway
The team discovered large-scale synthetic setups using proxies, VPNs and rented IP ranges. Recalibration is intended to remove synthetic data and rebalance rewards toward real devices.

Noto Nodes, [Apr 9, 2026 at 10:16]
#3
Customers pushed for network cleanup

Quote
“When customers see 10,000 VPN clients over a network… this is a scary sight for a customer.”

Takeaway
Enterprise partners apparently raised concerns about the authenticity of telemetry data. Cleaning the network is necessary to maintain credibility with telecom clients.

Noto Nodes, [Apr 9, 2026 at 10:17]
#4
Rewards for legitimate setups should return

Quote
“You can expect rewards for normal devices that are not synthetic… to restore in due course.”

Takeaway
The expectation set by the team is that legitimate devices should see rewards return once recalibration is finished.

Noto Nodes, [Apr 9, 2026 at 10:17]
#5
No confirmed timeline for recalibration

Quote
“It could be seven to ten more days… it could be four days.”

Takeaway
There is no firm date for when recalibration will end.

Noto Nodes, [Apr 9, 2026 at 10:17]
#6
SMS and CLI tasks already have contracts

Quote
“Contracts are already in place… the customer simply hasn’t started the volume yet.”

Takeaway
The team claims the upcoming SMS and CLI tasks already have commercial agreements. The missing piece right now is volume activation by customers.

Noto Nodes, [Apr 9, 2026 at 10:17]
#7
Scout & Runner is seen as a major revenue opportunity

Quote
“The impact can be enormous… very valuable to telecom carriers.”

Takeaway
The team is prioritising Scout & Runner because they believe it could generate significant telecom testing demand.

Noto Nodes, [Apr 9, 2026 at 10:18]
#8
Many additional tasks are planned

Quote
“I think there are about 60 tasks planned for the network.”

and:

“one third-party task will have insane volume”


Takeaway
Unity intends to expand beyond telemetry into a broader multi-task edge network, though most of these tasks are still roadmap items.
