Gwailow, [Jan 30, 2026 at 05:07]
I have a different viewpoint on this which is where my concerns stem from. 

The default, official (and currently only) marketplace is establishing a common financial arrangement for leases through the marketplace that limits the free choice of the potential ULO and limits competition amongst UNOs. For a regular person interested in becoming a ULO, their first port of call is likely to that marketplace as its directly available from the app - so its difficult to say that the ULO has any choice on the financial aspect of any lease they take through the marketplace.  Saying that UNOs can set their own terms is fine but this is looking at it from a UNO perspective - someone who just downloads the app and wants a license is unlikely to be in a position where they can contact a bunch of UNOs to get the best deal for them.

The danger here is that everyone is thinking from the perspective of a UNO and not from the potential ULO. It would be hard to say that setting a minimum does not limit competition or the freedom to choose from the ULO perspective. The one argument that I think could stand up is whether or not this is a financial arrangement but its a very grey area given that UPs are convertible to USD.
