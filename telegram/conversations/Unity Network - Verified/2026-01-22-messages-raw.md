Hello people. I have read all messages regarding the marketplace and the minimum share. Let me give you my two cents. I believe its wrong for us UNOs to argue regarding what is going to happen to the marketplace. Simply because, at up until now, at least, we do not have any kind of actual useful data to work with. The team knows and configures everything. The supply (tasks) but also they have the full dataset of the demand (leases, type of devices, locations - so can easily correlate leases with average wage etc).

All it takes for the team is a couple of smart marketeers to dive into the data and regulate the market so as to achieve the best possible  performance. I believe they should run some models and decide on an algorithm which will update the UNO/ULO dynamically based on market structure/KPIs (e.g. percent of leased/unleased licenses, type of devices (wifi only, sim enabled etc), farms or physical devices, ULO locations and of course type and number of tasks available at each time). So the marketplace, for me, should be a regulated market. A dynamic UNO/ULO split should be applied for any new license a ULO is taking from the marketplace. UNOs will not be able to change that for their licenses available on the marketplace. Of course we will be free to take our licenses off the marketplace and apply whatever split we like for our own ULOs.
