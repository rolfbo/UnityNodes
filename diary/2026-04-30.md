### 3. Phase 3: Analysis Tools ✅

#### Created detect_recurring_questions.py (373 lines)

**What it does:**
- Scans conversation archives for questions
- Detects patterns: ends with `?`, starts with question words
- Normalizes text for comparison
- Groups similar questions
- Reports frequency with emoji indicators (💬/💬💬/💬💬💬)

**Features:**
- Configurable threshold (default: asked 3+ times)
- Date range filtering
- Channel-specific or all-channels
- Shows example askers and date ranges

**Output example:**
```
💬💬💬 Asked 15 times:
   "when will ios app be available"
   Dates: 2026-04-01 to 2026-04-28
   Example: CommunityMember123
```

**Use case:** Automatically identify FAQ candidates instead of manual reading

---

#### Created detect_promises.py (434 lines)

**What it does:**
- Scans messages from known team members
- Detects commitment language patterns (20+ patterns)
- Categorizes by type:
  - 🎯 Commitments (will ship/release)
  - 📅 Timeline (coming soon, Q1/Q2, dates)
  - 🔵 In Progress (working on, in development)
  - 📢 Announcements (introducing, new feature)
  - ✅ Confirmed (promise, guarantee)

**Features:**
- Team member filtering (15 known team members)
- Signal strength tracking
- JSON export for processing
- Date range filtering
- Channel-specific or all-channels

**Output example:**
```
🎯 Commitments — 12 detected

[2026-04-28] Sophie (3 signals):
  "iOS app will ship by end of Q2, currently in final testing"
  Signals: will ship, by end of, in final testing
```

**Use case:** Never miss a team commitment, auto-populate tasks/active.md
